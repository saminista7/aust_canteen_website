# AUST Canteen Management System

A web-based canteen management system developed to digitize and streamline daily food ordering and administrative operations at **Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST)**.

---

## Overview
The AUST Canteen Management System is designed to improve the efficiency of canteen operations by enabling online food ordering, centralized order tracking, and administrative control through a web interface.  
Users can place food orders remotely from anywhere on campus, while administrators can manage items, categories, and incoming orders in real time.

---

## Key Features
- Homepage with canteen overview
- Food categories and item listing
- Online food ordering
- Contact page
- Admin panel for managing food items and orders

---

## System Overview
The system provides administrators with full control over canteen operations through an admin panel. Orders placed by users are recorded and tracked digitally, allowing better coordination and monitoring of services.  
The platform is designed with simplicity in mind, focusing on usability rather than complex automation.
